Instructions

The following test consists of 40 trials to test memory in different conditions. Each trial consists of a face which will be shown for a short period. There will be a blank screen, after which you will have to recall and click on the correct one shown in a series of other faces.

You can use the right mouse button to select the correct face. Please ensure that you complete the entire test. A message will be shown upon completion of the test. Please enter your initials in the box below and click on 'Start' to begin.
